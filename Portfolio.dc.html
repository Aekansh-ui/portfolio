06 / ABOUT

Engineer at heart, builder by habit.

I'm a data engineer from VIT Vellore. Starting at Cognizant in 2020, I've grown through NSE IT, SMRT Systems, and now Clairvoyant (EXL Company), where I build enterprise-scale data pipelines and ML orchestration platforms for American Express.

Across 5+ years — at Cognizant I worked with a healthcare client to develop an ETL pipeline on AWS and Snowflake using Medallion Architecture, at NSE IT I built SEBI regulatory pipelines and a GCP-to-AWS migration solution, and now enterprise merchant data infrastructure and RAG-based AI solutions at AmEx. My approach is consistent: understand the data and the stakeholders, design for failure, and keep operations boring where it counts.

Outside of engineering, I led technical workshops as Vice Chairperson of SME VIT and organised events with the Fine Arts Club — I believe the best engineers combine technical depth with curiosity and genuine interest in the people they build for.
